About S & B Landscaping Supply

Family owned since 2002, serving South Florida with pride

Our Story

Since 2002, S & B Landscaping Supply has been the premier choice for landscaping contractors and homeowners throughout South Florida. What started as a small family business has grown into the largest supplier of landscaping materials for South Florida landscape contractors, with the best selection of decorative stone and cap rock in the region.

Located at 7100 Loxahatchee Road in Parkland, Florida, we've built our reputation on three core principles: quality products, reliable service, and competitive pricing. Our convenient hours are designed to serve you when you need us most.
